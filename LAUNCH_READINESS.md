## Risk Assessment

### High Risk
1. **OAuth misconfiguration** - Could break login for returning users
2. **Auth disabled on API** - Potential abuse/quota bypass
3. **Hardcoded credentials** - Security exposure

### Medium Risk
1. **Console.log pollution** - May expose internal logic
2. **Silent errors** - Users may get stuck without feedback
3. **localStorage without migration** - Data loss on schema changes

### Low Risk
1. **Hardcoded CSS** - Maintenance burden, not user-facing
2. **Incomplete features** - "Coming soon" acceptable for MVP
3. **Documentation gaps** - Developer friction, not user-facing
